Consulting * Training * Research About Us

Tasc Business Consulting and training is a 100% black owned company. We specialize in training, business consulting, and research. We offer training to government departments, municipalities, private institutions and NGOs. Our courses and services assist our clients to enable and empower employees to become competitive differentiators for their company. Backed with our energetic, dynamic and exemplarily team we have the ability to successfully give you the best service to suite your needs. We are recognized for our reliability and our years of experience within the industry has given us the knowledge that is needed to ensure that jobs are run professionally, to schedule and are cost effective. This gives our clients comfort, confidence and less stress when engaging in our services. Our highly skilled facilitators are backed by a professional administration team which further enhances our overall services. We take pride in our team that possesses a judicious blend of knowledge in training, business consulting and research. We strive for quality and excellence-to be above the rest.
